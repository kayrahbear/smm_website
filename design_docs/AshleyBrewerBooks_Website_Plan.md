# Ashley Brewer Books Website Plan
Domain: ashleybrewerbooks.com  
Project: Launch website for the Savannah Manor Mysteries book series  
Primary Book: The Great Ghost Mix Up  

## Goals

1. Introduce Ashley Brewer as a children's author
2. Promote and sell the book *The Great Ghost Mix Up*
3. Provide a hub for future books in the Savannah Manor Mysteries series
4. Share illustrations, characters, and world-building
5. Provide information for parents, teachers, and schools
6. Build an email list for future book launches

---

# Audience

Primary Audience:
- Parents of children ages 4–9
- Teachers
- Librarians
- Grandparents buying gifts

Secondary Audience:
- Kids who want to explore the characters and story world

---

# Brand Personality

The brand should feel:

- Whimsical
- Slightly spooky but cozy
- Autumn/Halloween vibes
- Friendly and adventurous
- Storybook-like
- Warm Southern farmhouse atmosphere

Think:
"Cozy spooky mystery for kids"

---

# Visual Inspiration

The illustrations suggest a tone similar to:
