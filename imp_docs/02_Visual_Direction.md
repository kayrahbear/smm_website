# Visual Direction

## Tone
- Cozy spooky
- Storybook whimsical
- Warm autumn mystery
- Friendly and child-centered

## Artwork signals
- Pumpkin orange
- Night sky blue
- Ghost cream
- Soft moss and lavender accents
- Painterly textures
- Flashlight beams and glow

## Hero concept
Use the hallway flashlight illustration as inspiration for the homepage hero:
- Dark background
- Light beam effect
- Warm CTA buttons appearing in the illuminated area
- Headline that feels adventurous but gentle

## Interactive idea to preserve
Meet the Family page:
- Family illustration as the main visual
- Click on each person or pet
- Open a modal with a short bio and fun fact
- Could later include voice, favorite clues, or favorite spooky things
